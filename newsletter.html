"The Beauty of a Brainless Wonder"

The Aquarium of Jellyfish is to educate the public of such a strange yet wonderful species. People can learn what isn't commonly known about a jellyfish and actually get to see the wide variety of different types. Unique/Popular/Invasive Jellyfish are being displayed in this wonderful Aquarium. Throughout the Aquarium, they are categorized by size and location from all over the world!
